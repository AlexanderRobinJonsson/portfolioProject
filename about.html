About Me

Hello! I'm Alexander Robin Jonsson. I am a front-end developer with a background in graphic design and electromechanical engineering.

I specialize in building user interfaces that are visually compelling and engineered for precision and reliability.

My journey into the digital world began with a three-year education in graphic design, where I developed a keen eye for aesthetics, branding, and user-centric communication. While I loved shaping the visual experience, I was driven to bring these designs to life with interactivity and function. This ambition led me to pursue a comprehensive diploma in Full Stack Development.

I undertook this program while working as an Electromechanical Technician. This parallel experience was invaluable, as my daily work with technical machines and schematics grounded my web development studies in the principles of engineering and systematic problem-solving.
